About Us

Redistributing Medications, Restoring Health

At Medico, we are dedicated to harnessing the power of community generosity to bridge the gap in healthcare access. Our Medicine Donation Program is designed to collect unused, unexpired medications and distribute them to underprivileged communities and healthcare facilities in need. By donating to Medico, you can help provide essential treatments to those who cannot afford them, support medical professionals in delivering quality care, and contribute to a healthier world.
